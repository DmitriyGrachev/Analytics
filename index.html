Essential Guitar Gear

What Every Guitarist Needs

Beyond the guitar itself, there are several accessories that every guitarist should have in their collection.

  • Guitar picks in various thicknesses
  • Electronic tuner or tuning app
  • Extra strings (always be prepared!)
  • Guitar strap for standing practice
  • Gig bag or case for protection
  • Amplifier (for electric guitars)
  • Effects pedals to shape your sound
